How It Works

Choose a Laboratory Service

Search and select from our extensive database of services for the exact the laboratory service that you need to get done.

Plan The Service

Once you select a service you will be prompted to select the timeframe, location and availability of the services. Based on our partner who you will be in contact with, you will be provided with the Standard Operating Procedures (SOP’s) of the given service to ensure the testing is performed to your specifications.

Send Your Sample

Print your shipping label and use any of our approved mail service providers to send your sample.

Receive Results

You will be able to track the status of your sample through the website portal and then receive results in a confidential manner via your account portal.
